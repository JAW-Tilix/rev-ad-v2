Success Story

Champion Energy are a supplier of gas and electricity to businesses within the UK. Champion took advantage of recent uncertainty in the energy market to review their current position and strategy for growth. Revenue Assurance worked with Champion to develop their ability to respond to competitive tenders and also their representation through digital channels. Revenue Assurance worked with Champion to understand their unique market position, the value of this position and how to capitalise upon this through their digital presence and tender submissions. Taking the theoretical through to a real world response Revenue Assurance successfully co-ordinated detailed technical responses to competitive open tender with a local authority.
